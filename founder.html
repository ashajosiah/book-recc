Words from our Founder!

"Hello, my name is Asha Josiah and I am the founder and face of Aether"

Welcome to Aether, where a passion for literature fuels our mission. Founded in 2024 by me, a 19-year-old book enthusiast, Aether is more than just a business; it's a haven for fellow readers seeking literary treasures. With a keen eye for captivating narratives and transformative ideas, I curate a selection of books that inspire, educate, and entertain. My dedication to the written word drives every aspect of Aether, ensuring that each recommendation speaks volumes. Join me on this literary journey, where the love for books knows no bounds.
